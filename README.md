## ğŸ“Œ Problem Statement

Agricultural supply chains suffer from price opacity, inefficient logistics, and heavy middlemen dominance. Farmers often receive low returns, while buyers and consumers face higher costs and reduced transparency.

Green Groves aims to solve this by creating a **farmer-first, transparent, and secure digital platform** that supports contract farming and direct trade.
